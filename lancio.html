Lancio

Web Agency · France & French-Speaking Europe

Replicating the Mizra model for the French market — with a fully automated lead pipeline and paid acquisition.

01 — Problem

French SMBs need websites. Finding them at scale is the hard part.

The French web agency market is fragmented and price-sensitive. SMBs in Lyon, Marseille, Bordeaux need sites at €650–€1,500 — a price point most agencies ignore. The opportunity is real. The challenge is acquisition: how do you reach thousands of prospects systematically without a sales team?

Fragmented market

No dominant player at the €650–€1,500 price point for French SMBs outside Paris.

Acquisition at scale

Manual outreach doesn't scale. You need automation — scraping, sequencing, targeting — to reach enough prospects to convert consistently.

Wrong audience

Initial Meta Ads campaigns were burning budget on a 65+ demographic with zero business intent. Targeting needed a full rebuild.

02 — Approach

Automate the top of funnel. Close manually.

Site Architecture

Built lancio.fr on Astro 4 with sector landing pages, Stripe payment links, and a blog automated via GitHub Actions. SEO-first structure from day one.

Automated Lead Pipeline

Built a full pipeline: Lobstr scrapes French business leads by city and sector → Brevo sends automated email sequences → Supabase stores every lead and interaction → GitHub Actions cron manages follow-ups.

Meta Ads Rebuild

Diagnosed failing campaigns: wrong audience (65+, no business intent). Rebuilt with manual targeting capped at 25–54, entrepreneur and SMB interest filters. Fixed the broken order button that was converting paid traffic to nothing.

Email Campaigns by City

Launched Brevo email campaigns targeting French cities by sector. Lyon campaigns hit 100% open rate on first sends. Pipeline reaching real metrics.
