Mizra

Web Agency · Israel

Building the infrastructure of an Israeli web agency from zero — before the first client signs.

01 — Problem

The Israeli SMB market is underserved and hard to reach.

Small businesses in Israel — restaurants, clinics, law firms, beauty salons, contractors — need professional websites but have no trusted local provider who speaks their language, understands their market, and delivers fast. Most agencies are overpriced, slow, or generic. Cold outreach doesn't work if the message sounds corporate. WhatsApp does — if you know how to write it.

No local trust

Israeli SMBs don't respond to cold emails. They respond to direct, human, Hebrew-first outreach.

Overpriced market

Most Tel Aviv agencies quote ₪8,000+ for a site a focused builder can ship in 3 days.

No pipeline

Without a system, outreach is noise. Every lead needs to be tracked, followed up, and converted automatically.

02 — Approach

Build the machine before you need it.

Market Segmentation

Identified 8 high-intent sectors: restaurants, cafes, beauty salons, clinics, lawyers, barbershops, contractors, sports coaches. Built a dedicated landing page for each.

Lead Pipeline

Scraped 9,595 local business leads via Lobstr. Imported into Supabase. Segmented by sector and city. Pipeline ready for systematic outreach.

WhatsApp Outreach System

Built WATI integration for WhatsApp outreach. Tested and iterated messaging — dropped corporate tone, switched to minimal conversational Hebrew, withheld mockup until prospect responds. Response rate improved significantly.

Back-Office Infrastructure

Built a full custom back-office at mizra-bo.vercel.app: invoicing, client onboarding, PDF proposal analyzer via Anthropic API, IL/FR section split. A system that runs without a team.

Field Sales Layer

Deployed Bethsabée — a Hebrew-speaking phone outreach agent on hourly rate + 20% commission. Every call tracked in CRM with gamification and Supabase persistence.
