Day 01 / 30grademywebsite.arcane-studio.comAudit Tool · SEO · Performance

GradeMyWebsite

Problem: Most founders have no idea how their site actually performs. PageSpeed exists but it's clinical, jargon-heavy, and gives you a score with no context. GradeMyWebsite gives you an honest audit in 10 seconds — performance, SEO, accessibility — with a grade that actually means something. How it works: 1. Paste any URL — No account, no signup, no friction. 2. 10-second audit — Pulls real performance data. 3. Actionable output — A grade with context. Tech: Vanilla JS frontend, Lighthouse API, Netlify deployment, CTA feeds Arcane lead pipeline. Result: Live on day 1. Used to grade arcane-studio.com itself before launch.
